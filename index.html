Hey! Name's Nadia and I'm a 21 year old GD student at Algonquin College. I'm friendly and a bundle of energy once you get to know me.

I came into design because I wanted to help people in my own way, and for years I've done that through drawing. Doesn't hurt that it's just really fun to do! I am a designer, but I'm also an artist. I almost always insert my own illustrations in most of my designs.

My brand is called NightOwl which is a nod to my habit of working on projects late into the night.
